Projects

Sport Highlight Extraction

Completed in COMS 6735: Visual Databases

Explored the issue of automatically extracting highlights from videos of sports game, focusing on hockey and baseball. Classified videos by their sport using KNN algorithm on the most dominant color. Then used OCR to identify at what points of the video the score changed. The beginning and ending of the highlights were determined either by the change in the motion between frames or by a pattern that consistently was used to display highlights.

Trading Commodity Futures

Completed in COMS 4995: Financial Software Systems

Worked with a partner on a semester long project to design and implement a trade capture and processing system for natural gas futures. Created a Node.js web application with the help of Express, a web development framework, to allow users to enter trade requests that were then stored in a MongoDB database.
